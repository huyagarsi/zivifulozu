Nigerian socialite and popular billionaire’s wife Shade Okoya has taken to social media to celebrate her son Jubril Okoya.

Shade Okoya, while sharing several photos of her family rejoicing with her son, Jubril, on the new milestone, described him as his first love.

She wrote: Happy birthday son @jubrilokoya my first love.

Kemifilani News recalls that Shade Okoya, in a recent post on her Instagram page, said that being a mother is more than giving birth to a child.

According to the billionaire’s wife, motherhood means carrying and caring for a life completely dependent on the woman’s survival.

She added that being a mother means building children’s self-esteem, supporting their dreams, and loving them unconditionally.

Share a beautiful photo of herself and children, she wrote: Being a mother means more than having given birth to a child. It’s loving and knowing a soul before you even see it. The Police Council on Friday, confirmed Usman Alkali Baba as the Inspector General of Police (IGP).

This comes almost two months after President Muhammadu Buhari approved his appointment.

Alkali was confirmed during a meeting of the council in Abuja presided over by Buhari.

The Minister of Police Affairs Maigari Dingyadi, speaking after te meeting, quoted Buhari as urging the IGP to justify the confidence reposed on him by reducing the rising wave of insecurity in the country.

Alkali takes over from the immediate past Inspector General of Police, Mohammed Adamu.
