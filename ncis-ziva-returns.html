NCIS Ziva Returns

Ziva David was one of the most popular characters on NCIS, and her departure in season 11 left fans disappointed. However, rumors of her return began to circulate after a cryptic message from actress Cote de Pablo, who plays Ziva, on social media. The rumors were confirmed when Ziva returned for a special episode in season 17, where it was revealed that she was alive and well.

When Does Ziva Come Back to NCIS?

Ziva’s return to NCIS was initially a one-off appearance in season 17, where she helped the team with a case that had a personal connection to her. However, fans were left wanting more, and it was announced that Ziva would return in season 18 for a more extended arc. Ziva’s storyline in season 18 revolved around her search for answers about a conspiracy that had been hounding her for years.

When Does Ziva David Return to NCIS?

After the success of her arc in season 18, fans were eager to know if Ziva would return for another season. It was confirmed that Ziva would appear in season 19 of NCIS, but her storyline was kept under wraps. However, it has been confirmed that Ziva’s return will be linked to a life-or-death situation.
