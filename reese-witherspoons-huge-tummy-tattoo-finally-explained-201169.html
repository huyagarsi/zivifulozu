L.A. Ink, starring Reese Witherspoon?

Over the summer, the squeaky-clean Oscar-winner, 35, showed off her edgier side — on her toned tummy — while vacationing with new husband Jim Toth and her kids Ava, 11, and Deacon, 7.

Clad in a bikini, the How Do You Know star debuted a sizable tattoo just above her bikini line: a star flanked by two small birds. Fans of the blonde, blue-eyed actress wondered what inspired Witherspoon's ink.

"She has had the star tattoo for a few years," a source close to Witherspoon tells Us Weekly. "After she and Jim got married, she added two birds — one to each side of the star."
