Highlights

  • Joe Alwyn has moved on and is reportedly upset with being labeled just "Taylor's ex-boyfriend".
  • Sources have revealed that he is angry with the press and Taylor for overshadowing his career.
  • The actor wants to stop being asked about his past relationship and focus on his upcoming projects.

Despite being over a year since Joe Alwyn and Taylor Swift's breakup was announced, people are still speculating about what happened. Swifties got to learn a little about the singer's side of things from cryptic messages in her songs, but it wasn't until a few days ago that Joe Alwyn shared his feelings.

The actor has apparently found someone new that he's dating and isn't thinking about Taylor Swift anymore.

As people wonder why he decided to speak about it now, sources have come forward to share a shocking side of the actor. Apparently, he's a lot more angry than he let on about the situation. The insider said that Joe is reportedly upset with the press painting him as just "Taylor's ex", and with Taylor herself for turning swifties against him.

Joe Alwyn Wants People To Stop Seeing Him As "Taylor's Ex"

Both Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn have remained mostly quiet about the true reasons behind their breakup (unless you count Taylor writing songs about it). Neither of them had directly referred to it until Joe Alwyn broke his silence a few days ago, shocking swifties by being so open about his feelings.

Many theories have been going around about their split, including one that cited Joe's alleged infidelity as the cause. The actor seemed to clear that up in an interview recently, but people still wonder what went on between them.

The speculation is reportedly wearing on the actor, and he's tired of their breakup overshadowing his career, as he wants to be known for more than just being Taylor Swift's ex-boyfriend. Apparently, that was the reason he chose to finally address their breakup.

"Joe wanted to get this over with once and for all so he can move forward with his life and his career,' a source told DailyMail.com. "He never wanted to say anything and was hoping it would all blow over, but it hasn't."

The source also claimed that he's upset with the singer for monetizing the end of their relationship. "Taylor has made a fortune off their breakup by forming an entire album around it. Meanwhile, he has been cast as 'Taylor's ex.'"

Joe Alwyn is seemingly requesting the press to stop bringing up his relationship with Taylor.

"Joe has moved on and is doing promotional work for his films and upcoming projects, so he decided he needed to get this out to move forward. He wants to be asked about his films, not about Taylor."

The Source Compared Joe Alwyn To Travis Kelce

In a very bold statement, the insider decided to compare Taylor Swift's relationship with Joe to her current romance with Travis Kelce. The source compared the way they react to the spotlight that follows the pop star wherever she goes, implying that the Kansas City Chiefs tight end is milking the relationship for attention.
