You can recharge your StarTimes subscription through the StarTimes Mobile App, online platforms, POS, ATM, and USSD codes. The subscription prices range from N6600 to N900.

StarTimes is one of the biggest multinational media companies in Africa. They provide the best digital terrestrial television services and satellite television services. As a giant in digital television technology, StarTimes provides various ways of recharging its bouquets and different subscription prices.

Since StarTimes started operations in 2010, they have been known to offer the most affordable PayTV rate in Nigeria. StarTimes recharge and subscription prices are structured to suit the rich and the lower class. They have the highest subscription price of N6600 and the lowest price of N900 in different categories of their bouquet.

How to Recharge StarTimes Online

There are different platforms to recharge StarTimes online. Most of these platforms connect you directly to StarTimes, while others are online agents that have the approval to recharge StarTimes. Below are some online platforms to recharge your StarTimes:

Recharge Startimes Using the VTpass Online Platform

With VTpass, StarTimes customers can recharge their decoder online. The step is fast and easy for customers to use. Below are the quick steps for recharging your Startimes decoder online:

  • Log in to the VTpass website or click here
  • Select the bouquet to recharge
  • Enter StarTimes smartcard number
  • Enter your phone number
  • Enter your email address
  • Enter the amount to recharge
  • Click to continue
  • Insert all the required payment details
  • Click on submit.

Internet Banking Applications

The internet banking platform has made the payment of bills easy for StarTimes users. That is why StarTimes embraced the use of internet banking platforms in recharging the decoder. This StarTimes recharge platform through the internet banking platform is made available to all banks in Nigeria.

To successfully recharge or access StarTimes subscription prices through the internet banking platform, follow the steps below:

  • Access your bank’s mobile banking platform
  • Scroll to Pay Bills and click on it
  • Tap on Cable TV, select StarTimes from the list of options made available
  • Select the StarTimes package you want to recharge
  • Insert your StarTimes IUC number
  • Enter the bank’s authentication PIN

How To Recharge Your StarTimes Using The Quickteller

This is another payment platform that is embraced by StarTimes as a means of recharging the decoder. The platform is designed to be safe and secure. To successfully recharge and view StarTimes subscription prices through the quick teller platform, follow the steps below:

  • Login to the quickteller website or click here

  • Select a service category by using the drop-down arrow

  • Fill in your Smartcard number, email address, phone number, and amount
  • Continue your payment by clicking Continue
  • Your StarTimes TV decoder and account will be reconnected within ten minutes following a successful transaction

StarTimes Subscription Code

With a mobile phone, one can recharge their StarTimes decoder, even without having access to the internet. The phone method was made possible with the adoption of USSD codes. The USSD codes can be used to recharge or continue the subscription of any StarTimes bouquet, regardless of price.

Different Subscription Codes for StarTimes Bouquets

There are quite a number of codes to be used in subscribing to Startimes bouquets. Most of these codes are managed by banks and other financial institutions. Below are some of the USSD codes to subscribe to StarTimes.

StarTimes Subscription Code for First Bank

To successfully subscribe to StarTimes using the first bank’s USSD code, follow the steps below:

  • Dial *894#
  • Scroll down or next to the option with bills and input the number representing it
  • Insert your StarTimes IUC number
  • Select the bouquet
  • Finally, insert your transfer PIN

You can also use the First Bank USSD code to subscribe by dialing *894* Amount* IUC Number # and then entering the transfer PIN.

StarTimes Subscription Code for Access Bank

  • From your device, dial *901# and choose Option 4 for Other Services
  • Additionally, select 1 for Bill Payments and input the number for your cable TV subscription as seen on your screen
  • Follow the on-screen instructions after entering your smartcard or IUC number
  • Before you make a payment, double-check your input one final time

You can also dial *901*IUC Number*Amount#, and then input the transfer PIN.

StarTimes Subscription Code for FCMB

FCMB provided a standard USSD code for subscribing to StarTimes bouquets. To do this, simply dial *322*214*IUC number*Amount#, then input your PIN.

StarTimes Subscription Code for GTBank

Guarantee Trust Bank customers can subscribe to their StarTimes package using the USSD code. The short way of doing this subscription is to dial *737*37*amount*Smartcard or IUC number#, then confirm with your PIN.

StarTimes Subscription Code for Union Bank

Union Bank customers are not left behind in subscribing to StarTimes using the USSD code. Below are the steps to subscribing to StarTimes using the Union Bank USSD code:

  • *826# is the number to dial
  • Scroll to the option with bills and type in the number that represents it
  • Enter your StarTimes IUC number here
  • Choose a bouquet
  • Finally, enter your PIN for confirmation

StarTimes Subscription Code for Zenith Bank

  • Dial *966# using your mobile phone
  • Dial 8 to move to the next option list
  • To select bills, enter 12 into the input box.
  • Enter your IUC number
  • Enter the amount of the bouquet to subscribe
  • Then insert your transfer PIN

Customers can simply subscribe by dialing *966*7*Amount*IUC number#, then insert PIN.

StarTimes Subscription with Interswitch USSD Code

Before you begin, keep in mind that it will only work if you use the phone number associated with your bank account. How to use an Interswitch USSD code to pay for Startimes subscription packages;

From your phone, dial the shortcode *322*1*subscription code*amount #.

StarTimes Subscription Using the ATM

To recharge your Startimes subscription at an ATM machine, follow the procedures below. It’s worth noting that only ATMs marked with the Quickteller logo may be used to pay bills, including Startimes subscription. Here’s how to use an ATM to top up your Startimes account:

  • Insert your bank card—Verve, MasterCard, etc.—first
  • Click PROCEED after entering your ATM card password
  • Select Quickteller and then Pay
  • Bill from the drop-down menu
  • Select the sort of bank account you’re using—either savings, current, or, if applicable, the Merchant Code
  • The merchant code to enter for Startimes users in Nigeria is 240534
  • A screen appears that asks for a customer reference
  • This is where you enter your Startimes Smartcard number, after which a screen with the payment amount displays
  • Input the desired payment amount, which must be precise for current Startimes Bouquet pricing or in multiples of that amount that will run in months
  • Follow the on-screen steps to finish the purchase, and you’ll get a confirmation screen
  • You will get a message confirming your subscription

How To Subscribe StarTimes Through POS

POS can be used to pay for your StarTimes subscription. If you choose this method of payment, the POS representative will assist you. Following these steps will allow you to pay for your StarTimes subscription using a POS machine:

  • Put your ATM card in the slot and input the card pin
  • Choose PayArena from the list of alternatives
  • Select the option to pay bills and the bank account type for the ATM card
  • Choose DSTV from the list of possible billers
  • Pick your favorite bouquet
  • Enter your phone number as well as the smartcard number
  • To finish the purchase, choose yes
