What is Amazon StyleSnap? How does it work? We’re here to answer all of your questions, using J. Lo’s dress as an example. The service was created for fashion influencers to make their looks more accessible to followers, but we can also use it to find celeb-worn pieces or super similar styles. All it takes to get started is a screenshot!

First, simply take a screenshot of a photo featuring the piece you’re looking for, open up the Amazon app on your phone and click the camera icon (shown above) next to the search bar. You’ll then see an “Upload a Photo” button (shown below), so get it uploading that screenshot! Once the photo is uploaded, StyleSnap will do a quick digital search (5 to 10 seconds) of the image to find and identify products. You will see dots appear on the image as this is being done.

And then, voila! Scroll down to see your recommendations. You might find the exact piece you were looking for, but you also might find some great alternatives. With J. Lo’s dress, we were definitely looking for more of a look-alike style, and we found multiple affordable options. Amazon has so many clothing brands, you’re pretty much bound to find something you’ll love!

Don’t forget that once your results pop up, you can filter as you normally would to narrow things down. Filter by gender, price and if it’s on Amazon Prime, and then sort your prices from low to high, or sort based on customer reviews or newest arrivals. Amazon makes it so easy to find what you’re looking for. This tool is seriously just the thing to transform your closet, so try it out today and see what awesome pieces you find!
