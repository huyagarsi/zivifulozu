Ludovico Tersigni Biography – Ludovico Tersigni Wiki

Ludovico Tersigni is an Italian actor known for Slam 2016, SKAM Italia 2018, and Summertime 2016. He is known to the general public for his role as Stefano Privitera in the RAI Tutto series, Samuele in the film Slam – Everything for a Girl and, from 2018, known for the role of Giovanni Garau in Skam Italia. He graduated from the Classical High School Chris Cappell College in Anzio. From middle school, he has ventured into musicals, continuing to study in musical companies and attending theater courses. In 2014, he made his film debut in the film Arance & martello directed by Diego Bianchi, presented at the 71st Venice International Film Art Exhibition. After two years, he obtained a secondary role in L’estate addosso, a film directed by Gabriele Muccino. He appeared in the Italian series Tutto è succire in 2015, playing the character of Stefano Privitera.

He was chosen to play the role of the protagonist in the film Slam – All for a girl in 2016, presented at the Turin Film Festival, and then distributed in theaters the following year. In order to play the character Samuele, the protagonist of the film, he has learned to go skateboarding, thus creating firsthand some of the evolutions foreseen in the film. In 2018 he made his debut as Giovanni Garau in the Skam Italia web series. He was awarded the 2017 – Guglielmo Biraghi Award for Slam – All for a girl with the Silver Ribbons 2017.
