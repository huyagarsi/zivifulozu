Ciryl Gane- Wiki, Biography, Age, Height, Net Worth, Wife

Ciryl Gane is a renowned French professional mixed martial artist who has gained recognition for his exceptional career in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) Heavyweight division, culminating in his former Interim UFC Heavyweight Champion title. His prominence as one of the top heavyweight fighters globally has been solidified with his prior victories in TKO Major League MMA, where he held the Heavyweight Champion title from 2014.

Quick Facts


Ciryl Gane Age, Biography

Ciryl Gane, born Ciryl Jacky Gane, entered the world on April 12, 1990, in the French town of La Roche-sur-Yon. Raised in his hometown, La Roche-sur-Yon, Ciryl has both French nationality and Guadeloupean ancestry through his father. As of 2024, he commemorated his 34th birthday. He hails from a Christian family background.

His father, Romain Gane, held positions as a bus driver and Division d’Honneur footballer. During his youth, Gane actively participated in football and basketball. Despite his athletic prowess, Gane pursued sales at a furniture store and engaged in a work-study program in Paris. His educational background comprises graduation from a prestigious high school or university.

Ciryl Gane Height & Weight
