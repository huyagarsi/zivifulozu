Viktor Hovland Parents: Who are Viktor Hovlands parents?

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  170 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Viktor Hovland is a Norwegian professional golfer who plays on the PGA Tour. He turned professional following the 2019 U.S. Open, and made his professional debut at the Travelers Championship in June. By doing so, he forfeited his automatic entry to the 2019 Open Championship.

Hovland was born on 18 September 1997 to parents Galina Hovland and Harald Hovland.

Who are Viktor Hovland’s parents?

Harald Hovland

According to reports, Viktor’s dad is a wealthy finance manager who runs a privately owned business in the United States.

Galina Hovland

According to reports, Galina was more focused on the kids so she was a home maker.

There’s not much information on Viktor’s parents.

Viktor Hovland PGA Tour

He won the 2018 United States Amateur Championship and then became the first Norwegian to win on the PGA Tour (at the 2020 Puerto Rico Open) and on the European Tour (at the 2021 BMW International Open).

He picked up his second and third PGA Tour wins at the 2020 and 2021 Mayakoba Golf Classic.
